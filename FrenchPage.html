FRENCH

Circa 842 A.D.

Ah, French. The language of love. Beyond baguettes and cheese, however, French speakers enjoy all things delicious. With nearly 275 million native speakers, French is recognized is the 6th most widely spoken language in the world! French is an official language in 29 countries, spanning 5 continents, making it a linguistic powerhouse! So start learning French today with a Buddy and Voilà, you'll be speaking in no time!

HOW FRENCH SOUNDS

Perhaps what French is most known for is it's smooth, romantic sounding nature. French does have some sounds though that have notoriously been tricky for foreigners, such as the gutteral "r" and the exaggerated "u" sounds. On top of this, French is not a phonetic language, meaning that words are not pronounced as they are spelled, often making it difficult to follow when written. Don't worry though, French does follow many grammatical patterns that once you learn make it much easier to follow when written. No one said learning a language was going to be easy, but with all of our Language Buddy resources and listening to the way French speakers sound, who wouldn't want to learn French!?

Accents and Regions


Given that the Franch truly colonized the world, claiming territory in places like: the Caribbean, the South Pacific, the Indian Ocean, and much of Africa, the French language continues to be spoken in all corners of the globe. Naturally, as French culture and the language began to enter these remote parts of the world, there now exists a plethora of different accents and dialects of the French language, making it one of the most diverse cultures on the planet! Learning French will enable you to converse with much of the world!
