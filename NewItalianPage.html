Quick Tips French:

How do you pronounce the ç?

  • The 'ç' is pronounced as an 's'. Ex: Garçon.

    • How do you pronounce the 'r'?

  • The 'r' in French is soft and at the back of the throat. Ex: Rue

    • Do accent marks mean you put the emphasis on that vowel?

  • No. The accent mark in French tells you how to pronounce the vowel. Ex: Protégé

    • How do you pronounce the Accent Grave?

  • The accent grave is meant to be pronounced more open. Ex: Mère, première
