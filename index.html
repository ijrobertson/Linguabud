WHY LANGUAGE BUDDY?


Ever get frustrated because you want to speak a new language but don't even know where to begin? Or do you say: "I studied Spanish for 4 years in high school and still can't follow a basic conversation!"? If so, don't worry, you are not alone! One of the biggest reasons why we fail to learn languages is due to motivation. Without someone else pushing us to stay motivated, it can be hard to keep your eye on the prize. That is why making friends who are just as motivated as you can be your final ticket to fluency! Think about it: Do you workout as hard at the gym alone as with a buddy? Just like working out your muscles, you need to "workout" your mind when learning a new language. Speaking everyday for just 10 minutes can get you to fluency much faster than studying in a class and having a buddy makes it fun! Plus, it's free, so what do you have to lose!

Not sure how to get started? Don't worry, Language Buddy gives you a plethora of resources to help you get started with your new buddy! Make sure to check out our invaluable Resources page to help you start speaking a new language today!
